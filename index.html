• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • 1 tbsp of butter
    • Step 1: Spread butter on one side of each bread slice.
    • Step 2: Place cheese slices between the buttered sides of the bread.
    • Step 3: Grill the sandwich until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted.
  1. Step 1: Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Step 2: Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Step 3: Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Step 4: Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Step 5: Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
